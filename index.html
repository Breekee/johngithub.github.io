Rush

Rush is a Canadian rock band. Despite comprising of only three members, they bring a full sound. Alex Lifeson (guitar), Geddy Lee (bass and vocals), and Neil Peart (drums) have played together since 1974, until Peart's death in 2020. Their most famous song is Tom Sawyer, and they got lyrics about how trees are talking to each other, how different sides of the brain works, and outer space stuff. Alex and Geddy are gearing up for a tour this year.
